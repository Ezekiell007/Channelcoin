# Code of Conduct – channelGold (CHG)

## Our Commitment

As contributors and maintainers of the **channelGold (CHG)** project, we are committed to creating a welcoming, safe, and inclusive environment for everyone.  
We value respect, professionalism, and constructive collaboration.

This Code of Conduct outlines expectations for participants and explains how to report unacceptable behavior.

---

## Standards of Behavior

### Expected Behavior
Participants should:

- Treat everyone with respect and kindness  
- Use welcoming and inclusive language  
- Provide constructive feedback  
- Accept criticism gracefully  
- Focus on what is best for the project  
- Show empathy toward other community members  

### Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments  
- Personal or political attacks  
- Public or private threats  
- Sharing others’ private information without permission  
- Disruptive behavior or trolling  
- Any action that harms the project or community  

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying behavior standards  
- Reviewing and addressing issues professionally  
- Taking appropriate actions when rules are violated  
- Removing or banning contributors who engage in harmful behavior

## Enforcement Guidelines

1. **Warning** – A private reminder about the Code of Conduct  
2. **Temporary ban** – Short-term removal from discussions or contributions  
3. **Permanent ban** – Removal from the project for repeated or severe violations

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Contributions in the GitHub repository  
- Community platforms (Telegram, Twitter, etc.)  
- Any interactions related to the channelGold project
