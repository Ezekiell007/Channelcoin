# channelGold (CHG) Use Cases

CHG can be used in various ways within and outside the channelGold ecosystem.

---

## 1. Payments in Stores and Services

- Partner stores can accept CHG as payment.  
- Customers pay by scanning a QR code from the official CHG wallet.  
- Incentives such as **discounts for CHG payments** can be applied.

---

## 2. Games and Apps

- CHG can be used as **in-game currency** within channelGold games.  
- Players can earn CHG by completing challenges or purchasing digital items/NFTs.  
- This creates real demand and increases community engagement.

---

## 3. Transactions and Transfers

- Sending CHG between users is **fast and low-cost** using the Solana blockchain.  
- Can be used for **microtransactions or online payments**.  
- Facilitates integration into digital marketplaces.
