# Staking channelGold (CHG)

Staking allows you to **lock your CHG tokens** to support the network and earn **rewards**.

---

## How to Stake

1. Open your Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom).  
2. Make sure you have enough CHG tokens.  
3. Access the official channelGold staking portal.  
4. Choose the amount of CHG you want to lock and confirm.  
5. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the Solana blockchain.

---

## Rewards

- Rewards are distributed proportionally to the amount of CHG staked.  
- Pool fees may vary depending on protocol updates.  
- Tokens in staking continue to participate in governance votes.

---

## Liquidity Pools

- Liquidity pools allow you to **provide CHG together with another cryptocurrency** (e.g., SOL) to facilitate trading.  
- In return, you earn **fees and additional rewards**.  
- Check the official portal for active pools and details.
