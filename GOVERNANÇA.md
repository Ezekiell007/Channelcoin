# channelGold (CHG) Governance

This document defines the **governance structure**, community participation, and rules for decision-making related to the **channelGold (CHG) cryptocurrency**.

---

## 1. Decision Structure

The governance of channelGold is composed of:

- **Developers Committee**: responsible for technical updates, token maintenance, and feature integration.  
- **Community**: participates through voting on major proposals.

---

## 2. Community Participation

All CHG holders can take part in decisions through:

- **Improvement Proposals**: any user can suggest new features or changes.  
- **Voting**: strategic decisions are voted on by the community, with voting power proportional to the amount of CHG staked.

---

## 3. Proposal Rules

To be valid, a proposal must:

1. Be submitted in writing on official community channels (Telegram, Discord, or official forum).  
2. Include objective, expected impact, and cost estimate (if applicable).  
3. After submission, the proposal will be **open for discussion and voting** for a defined period.

---

## 4. Token Updates

Decisions regarding the token include:

- Adjustments to **staking and liquidity pools**.  
- Updates to **CHG token features** or the **channelGold ecosystem**.  
- Strategic changes affecting token distribution or tokenomics.

> All decisions must be **documented and communicated** to the community.

---

## 5. Transparency

- All votes and results will be **publicly recorded and shared**.  
- Significant changes to the token or project will only be implemented **with community approval** and official documentation.  
- Periodic reports on decisions and updates will be available in the repository and official channels.

---

## 6. Document Updates

The `GOVERNANCE.md` file can be updated as the project evolves, as long as all changes follow the **proposal and voting process** described above.
