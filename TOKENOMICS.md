# ChannelGold (CHG) — Tokenomics

## 1. Overview
The ChannelGold (CHG) token follows a simple, transparent, and community-focused tokenomic model.  
All tokens are created at launch and distributed entirely to the public, ensuring a fair and open ecosystem.

---

## 2. Token Details
- **Token Name:** ChannelGold  
- **Symbol:** CHG  
- **Blockchain:** Solana  
- **Standard:** SPL  
- **Decimals:** 9  
- **Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 CHG  

---

## 3. Token Distribution
ChannelGold uses a **100% community allocation model**, meaning no tokens are reserved for the team, founders, or private investors.

### **Distribution Breakdown**
| Category | Allocation | Amount |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Public Supply | 100% | 1,000,000,000 CHG |

All tokens are available for trading, liquidity, and community use.  
No hidden allocations, no pre-mines, no locked team wallets.

---

## 4. Liquidity
A portion of CHG will be paired with SOL to provide decentralized liquidity on Solana-based DEXs such as:

- Raydium  
- Orca  

Additional liquidity will be added over time as the project grows.

A liquidity lock (LP Lock) will also be applied to increase trust and stability.

---

## 5. Utility
CHG aims to serve multiple functions within its ecosystem, including:

- **Trading** — Accessible for all users on decentralized markets  
- **Staking (future)** — Rewarding holders with passive income  
- **Ecosystem Utility** — CHG may be used in future apps and platforms  
- **Community Rewards** — Incentives for supporters and contributors  

---

## 6. Sustainability Model
ChannelGold maintains value through:

- Fair launch community distribution  
- Transparent project development  
- Liquidity growth  
- Increasing utility over time  
- Long-term roadmap phases  

There are **no taxes**, **no burns** unless voted by the community, and **no automatic fees**.

---

## 7. Conclusion
The tokenomics of ChannelGold are designed to be simple, fair, and community-powered.  
With 100% of tokens available to the public and no team allocation, CHG positions itself as a transparent and trustworthy project on the Solana blockchain.
